What I do day to day

Most of my time goes into project coordination — gathering requirements from stakeholders, writing functional specs, planning timelines, and reporting progress to management. I run UAT cycles, coordinate user training, and keep cross-functional teams (ops, devs, vendors) moving in the same direction through the SDLC.

Technical foundation I lean on

On the technical side, I'm comfortable enough with AWS, Terraform, Docker, and GitHub Actions to set up and reason about infrastructure — not as a specialist, but enough to coordinate confidently with engineers, troubleshoot at a basic level, and ship small things myself (Linux servers, Nginx, FastAPI/Node.js, SQL databases). It's a supporting toolkit for the coordination work, not the main focus.
